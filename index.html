SUU'S RAHMA

About Us

Suu's Rahma Guest house is nestled in its own secluded tropical garden at East Airport, off Spintex road, creating the perfect setting for a relaxing holiday or business trips in Accra, Ghana. At Suu's Rahma the air is filled with a soothing smell of the garden, which sets the tone for the type of a harmonious and relaxing atmosphere our guests can expect to enjoy.

Find Us

The facility is strategically located with convenient access to amenities and facilities such as restaurants, shopping malls, banks, cultural centers, etc. Suu's Rahma is a 4km transfer from the Kotoka international airport, 2.1km from Palace Mall, 3.2 from Accra mall and 13km from the beach.

There is a restaurant that serves our guests when they need in house meal. We also have a Suites with self-catering and a lounge.
